Job Description

As an account executive, you will be reporting directly to the account manager, managing all the social media channels, campaigns, coordinating with the creative team, to ensure an engaging audience experience, and make sure our brands are delighted by us. You will be working directly with the client & be the executive level point of contact.

Responsibilities and Duties (May include but are not limited to)

  • Assisting with onboarding and streamlining clients' social media accounts (ex. Facebook, Instagram etc.)

  • Preparation of bi-weekly, and monthly drafts along with reports as per the requirements.

  • Act as the liaison between the client and our internal team, debriefing the internal team and ensuring the clientâ€™s demands are met.

  • Posting of content of social media channels as per the schedule, on consistent & relevant intervals.

  • Work with the art team on design and copy requirements as per the strategy, ensuring high quality feedback and end result.

  • Debrief ads team as per the client objectives, ensure high quality output from them and ensure that the relevant objectives are being met.

  • Look for and pitch campaign ideas and layered activities to your client; ensuring the revenue goals of the agency are met.

  • Be an active part of brainstorming sessions, contributing ideas and understanding the relevance of them as per the brand.

  • Keep an update on the latest trends in the digital industry, look for gaps for your clients and the overall company and actively contribute for improvement.

  • Assist in media planning and buying as per the client brief.

  • Handling any other operations as directed by the account management team/leadership team of the company.

Required Experience, Skills and Qualifications

  • Minimum 6 months of experience in a marketing/creative agency, working as a social media executive, account executive or a similar role in account operations.

  • Basic knowledge of performance marketing and media buying.

  • Excellent communication, negotiation and client handling skills.

  • Active user of social media channels on a personal level.

  • Good knowledge of design aesthetics, and experience working on Canva to design presentations, drafts etc.
