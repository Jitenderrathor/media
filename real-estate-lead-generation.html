Understanding Relevant Ads with Meta
In real estate, the most effective Meta Ads for lead generation fall into two categories: On-Platform Leads and Off-Platform Leads. Each serves a distinct purpose and offers unique benefits.
On-Platform Leads
  • On-Platform Lead Ads capture user information directly within Meta, providing a seamless and cost-effective experience.
  • 1. Instant Forms: Pre-filled forms with user details make it easy and quick for leads to submit information, boosting conversion rates.
  • 2. Custom Questions: Tailor forms with questions about budget, property preferences, and timing to qualify leads better.
  • 3. Lower Cost: On-platform leads are generally cheaper due to reduced user friction and higher submission rates.
  • Example: A user clicks on an ad, fills out a quick form, and submits their detailsâ€”all within the Meta platform.
Landing Page Leads
Landing Page Lead Ads direct users to a dedicated landing page, ideal for providing detailed information and capturing higher-quality leads.
  • 1. Custom Landing Pages: Control the content and design, offering a richer experience with virtual tours, detailed property info, and lead magnets.
  • 2. Higher Quality Leads: Although more expensive, these leads are often more serious as they've taken extra steps to engage.
  • 3. Enhanced Content: Use the landing page to offer valuable content like downloadable brochures or market reports to entice users.
Example: A user clicks on an ad and is taken to a landing page with in-depth property details, where they fill out a form to get more information.
On-Platform Leads are cheaper and great for quick conversions, while Landing Page Leads offer higher quality by engaging users more deeply. Balancing both can help you maximize your real estate marketing efforts.
